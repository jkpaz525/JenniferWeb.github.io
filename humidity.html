Humidity

This diagram display the relationship between city latitude and humidity for January 5, 2017. As you will note, latitude is listed along the x-axis and the percentage of humidity is listed along the y-axis. The majority of the high humidity percentage is along the -20 - 70 degrees latitude at 100% humidity.
